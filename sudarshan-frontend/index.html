Our Story
Building
Gujarat's
Future

Founded in 2000 by Nagjibhai S. Barot, Sudarsan Infracom has risen as Gujarat's most innovative construction company. Our journey began from a simple office building and has now reached mega smart city projects.

Our vision is clear — blending technology and tradition to build structures that make future generations proud. From GIFT City to Dahej, our fingerprint is present in every corner of Gujarat.

In a journey of 25+ years, our 140+ projects and presence across 12 cities are our greatest awards.
